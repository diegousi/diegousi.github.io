Expertise: Data Science and Machine Learning

I am a currently pursuing a Masters in Artificial Intelligence at Università della Svizzera italiana in Lugano. I hold a B.Sc. (2014) in Applied Mathematics from ITAM, Mexico City, Mexico. In addition to that, I have 7 years of experience working as Data Scientist and Machine Learning Engineer in the tech industry, mainly in startups directly impacting organizations through models and data.

My primary research interests are Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision and Neuroscience, particularly Neuro and Bio inspired algorithms and Deep Learning for intelligent systems. I believe human brain, as well as decentralized groups of organisms in nature, are to be understood in order to create new smart systems for all realms.

Academic Publications

B.Sc. Thesis: Programming with neural networks (Spanish only)

In this work, I replicate the work done in pix2code: given the gui of a web app, a hybrid convolutional - recurrent model infers the code necessary for the given input. As well, I build the mathematical foundation of neural networks from scratch, incluiding convolutional and LSTM layers. Furthermore, I propose alternative architectures, that include attention mechanisms, to solve the problem with better results.
